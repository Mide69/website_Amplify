About TEK Tribe - Tech for Good Startup

We believe technology should be a force for good, driving positive social and environmental change across the UK.

At TEK Tribe, we are committed to using our IT cloud solutions expertise to drive positive social and environmental change. Our business operates with a strong community focus, ensuring that innovation serves not just profit, but people and the planet.

As a CIC/CSR-driven business, we embed social impact into our core operations, creating a movement to make technology equitable, sustainable, and transformative for all.
